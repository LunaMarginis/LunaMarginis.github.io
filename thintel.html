Cyber Threat Intelligence

These resources deals with the information about threats and threat actors that helps mitigate harmful events in cyberspace.

CTI is used to prepare, prevent, and identify cyber threats looking to take advantage of valuable resources.


  • X-Force Exchange by IBM

    IBM X-Force Exchange is a threat intelligence sharing platform that you can use to research security threats, to aggregate intelligence, and to collaborate with peers.

  • OTX by AlienVault®

    AlienVault’s Open Threat Exchange (OTX) gives a good idea of where threats are coming from within organization, which is often difficult to pinpoint.



  • URLhaus Database by Abuse(.ch)

    URLhaus is a project operated by abuse.ch. The purpose of the project is to collect, track and share malware URLs, helping network administrators and security analysts to protect their network and customers from cyber threats.



  • Cyber Threat Maps

    Threat maps can be used to study past attack styles, to recognize raw data behind DDoS attacks, or to even report outages on certain dates and times to their customer base

  • MISP

    A threat intelligence platform for sharing, storing and correlating Indicators of Compromise of targeted attacks, threat intelligence, financial fraud information, vulnerability information or even counter-terrorism information.


  • VirusTotal

    Search VirusTotal's dataset for malware samples, URLs, domains and IP addresses according to binary properties, antivirus detection verdicts, static features, behavior patterns such as communication with specific hosts or IP addresses, submission metadata and many other notions. Pinpoint files similar to your suspect being studied. Samples matching search criteria can be downloaded for further study.
